The Home to Games

A small 3-level arcade project — jump, fall, then align the tiles.

How the Game Works

  1. Level 1 (Jump) — Click anywhere (or tap) to jump. Need score: 3
  2. Level 2 (Fall) — Use left / right arrow keys to dodge holes. Need score: 15
  3. Level 3 (Align) — Stack/align sliders. Need successful stacks: 10

Unlimited tries on levels 1 & 2. Final level: one run — fail and you'll go to the Lose screen.
